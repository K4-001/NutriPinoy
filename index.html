About NutriPinoy

NutriPinoy is a web-based project that presents nutrition information for common Filipino dishes in a clear and accessible way. It helps users understand ingredients, estimated calories per serving, macronutrient content, and possible health considerations when certain foods are eaten frequently.

The project focuses on awareness and moderation rather than restriction, encouraging healthier choices while respecting Filipino food culture. NutriPinoy was developed as a portfolio project to demonstrate skills in web development, data organization, and user-centered design, with a structure built to support future API integration for dynamic and scalable nutrition data.

  • Highlights the nutrients and ingredients of Filipino meals.
  • Encourages healthy eating without giving up flavor.
  • Built with love for Pinoy food and student wellness.
